Home Repair Cost Estimator

This site is designed to help give budgetary estimates for residential repair and improvement project cost. It is not designed to give fixed pricing of what it will be to have repairs or replacements done. For that you will need to contact a qualifies contractor and get a bid for project pricing. The intent of this tool is to help take into consideration some of the factors that will influence the price of the job. Often a homeowner is not familiar with factors that impact pricing and hopefully, this will give them a better idea of what goes into the cost.
